A JOYFUL STAY IN THE CITY OF JOY

One could either see the city of joy - Kolkata, as a major reflection of the colonial era with its centuries-old buildings, British monuments, and landscaped parks, or as a land that has given birth to generations of heart-felt poets, writers, and film directors. No matter which way you look at it, the deep Bengali love of culture and the triumph of art will fill your heart.

The Lindsay welcomes you to this city of joy and colourful cultures with immense pleasure. Experience this vibrant city while residing at one of the best hotels near Park Street that gloriously celebrates the union of the new and the old. Located in a prime area near New Market, it is one of the best hotels in Kolkata that features a rare combination of old-world charm in its design and contemporary living in its services and style. The Lindsay offers beautiful rooms decorated with soothing interiors and is well equipped with modern amenities. The windows of these rooms open up to alluring city views.

This hotel near Park Street and New Market comforts guests with the various flavours of Kolkata, at its rooftop restaurant and coffee shop. For those who wish to host fabulous events in an excellent location in the city with unmatched banqueting services, our Park Street hotel offers 7 splendid venues. Guests can take a walk to the nearby pubs and cafes,Eden Gardens,Victoria Memorial, and central Kolkata, which are all in close proximity. With warm hospitality and premium facilities, the hotel offers a whole new world of exciting and engaging stays!
