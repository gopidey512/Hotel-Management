BLUE & BEYOND - A HAPPENING ROOFTOP RESTAURANT IN KOLKATA
Escape the crowds and enjoy the endless skies for an interesting evening at Blue & Beyond, our rooftop resto-bar in Park street. Let the bright sunlight sparkle up your day and the glittering stars shimmer your evenings with enjoyment as you gorge on food to die for and drinks to spice things up. The cool breeze and the soft music in the air complement each other to set the perfect mood for you and your friends. As famous as it is among the local visitors, Blue & Beyond is one of the best rooftop restaurants in Park Street and is quite the buzz among travellers as well. Along with the bright open space, this restaurant in Park Street also has an adjoining intimate indoor A/C section. The design of the restaurant is inspired by the 5 elements of nature along with the gazebos that add to the overall charm. Visit Blue & Beyond for an epic experience!
Timings: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
This Multi-cuisine Bar & Restaurant with Live Kitchen, Kolkata's only venue with a fantastic 3600 view from the rooftop with inspirations taken from the 5 Elements of nature and Gazebos adding to the charm, serves favourites from World Cuisine.
At its unique open-air Interactive Bar, the mood turns intoxicating, setting the tone for conversation with friends as the bartender serves up an array of lager, spirits, cocktails, and an extensive range of wines and champagne at our restaurant in Kolkata.
Regularly featured in The Lonely Planet, this joint is a favourite with the foreign guests as they enjoy the sun during winter afternoons while sipping their favourite drink, including Draught Beer. The Five elements form the unique theme of this inimitable outlet. One can sit on a table as water flows under it and listen to live unplugged instrumental music. The tinkle of the chimes as they swing in the air adds to the charm and the wide-open spaces make you feel one with the elements.
HOGG'S ROOM - A COSY CAFE IN ESPLANADE
Hogg's Room is a throwback to the golden days! This fabulous cafe in Esplanade is a fusion coffee pub that brings the allure of the alfresco experience indoors. Embrace the charm of the olden days as you walk in and experience the modern lifestyle, as we serve the day's favourite beverages and snacks. From a fine collection of teas and coffees to the choicest of snacks, chilled lager, and a variety of wines and spirits, this is one of the best cafes in Esplanade where we indulge your taste buds with culinary delights.
Come experience a combination of nostalgia and coffee at Hogg’s Room!
Timings: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
